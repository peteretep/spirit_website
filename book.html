The MV Spirit is an ex-RNLI Fast Carriage Boat (FCB). She is the first Fast Carriage Boat to be sold and is the only one in private ownership. Recently fitted with new twin 320HP engines, she is certified to meet the most stringent passenger safety measures. The MV Spirit was the prototype FCB which later became the Mersey Class of lifeboat. The first Mersey Class Lifeboat went into service in 1989 and is still the state of the art Fast Carriage boat in the RNLI fleet.
The Skipper has many years experience in search and rescue missions and the SAR equipment has been retained.
