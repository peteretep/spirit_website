Being based in both Westport and Old Head, we can offer the best of both worlds.
Westport offers all the infrastructure of one of the busiest tourist towns in the country - hotels, pubs, restaurants and all the rest. On the water there are good fishing grounds in close proximity. Seals are common around some of the rocky Islets and the multitude of islands offer protection in all but the very worst of conditions.

Old Head is farther west and near the deeper waters, dolphins, porpoise, basking sharks and even whales are to be found in these waters. There is sand, "rough ground" and reef fishing on the door step, not to mention Clare Island within easy striking distance. While we will accommodate your party in any way we can, a selection of "standard" trips are listed below. We can mix and match to your requirements.

  • All day angling circa 8 hours €450/€550

  • Two and half hour evening trip, sight seeing, seals etc or angling €250

  • Half day as above €300

  • Two days, overnighting on an Island from €800

  • All above for up to 12 people. Occasionally smaller parties can be accommodated at €25 to €50 per head.
